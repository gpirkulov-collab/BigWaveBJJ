About the Academy

Big Wave BJJ is a Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu academy based in Budva, Montenegro. We offer professional training in a welcoming and supportive environment, guided by experienced black belt coaches with over 15 years on the mats.

Whether you are a complete beginner or a seasoned competitor, our programs are designed to help you grow. We welcome locals, expats, and travelers from around the world to train with us.

Our Programs

Training options for every level, age, and goal. Find the program that fits you.

BJJ Adults

Fundamentals and advanced techniques for all levels. Gi and no-gi classes with a focus on technique, strategy, and live sparring.

BJJ Kids

Fun and safe training for children from age 4. We build discipline, confidence, and coordination through age-appropriate drills and games.

Open Mat

Free sparring sessions and drilling. A chance to work on your game, try new techniques, and roll with training partners at your own pace.

Camps & Seminars

Intensive training camps with guest instructors from around the world. Combine world-class BJJ training with the beauty of the Montenegrin coast.

Corporate Training

Team building through martial arts. Boost morale, develop leadership skills, and create bonds that go beyond the office.
